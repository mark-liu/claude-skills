# company-research

Structured company research for interview and consulting preparation. Given a company name or links, produces a briefing covering overview, funding, leadership, strategy, culture, and recent news.

## Install

```bash
claude plugin install company-research@mark-liu-skills
```

## Contents

1 skill (`company-research`):

| Section | Coverage |
|---------|----------|
| Company Overview & Financials | Funding rounds, revenue/ARR, valuation, customers, competitive landscape |
| Leadership & Culture | Founders, C-suite, Glassdoor themes, engineering culture, work model |
| Strategy & News | Acquisitions, partnerships, SEC filings, earnings calls, controversies |
| Interview Angles | 3-5 informed questions based on research findings |

Uses 3 parallel research tracks (WebSearch + WebFetch) to cover financial, people, and strategy dimensions simultaneously.

## Trigger Phrases

- "research [company]"
- "tell me about [company]"
- "prep me on [company]"
- "company research [name]"
- Sharing company links before an interview

## Output

Markdown briefing document with: at-a-glance table, funding history, leadership profiles, product/market analysis, strategy, culture assessment, recent news timeline, SEC filings summary (public companies), and suggested interview questions.
